Voice & tone

70% comedian · 20% friend · 10% pro.

If the line sounds like it came from any other cleaning company, rewrite it. Punchy, self-aware, human.

✗ Never write this

  • "Affordable cleaning services."
  • "Reliable, professional cleaners."
  • "We care about your home."
  • "Our trained team of experts."
  • "At MaidThis, we believe…"

✓ Write this instead

  • "Real humans. Real clean. No clipboard energy."
  • "Sparkling floors. Zero sparkling effort."
  • "Your Airbnb, guest-ready in 3 hours."
  • "Your couch is judging you. Fix that."
  • "Nobody actually enjoys scrubbing grout."
