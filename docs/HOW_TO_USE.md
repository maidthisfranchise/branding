# How to use the MaidThis Brand Design skill

**You got this zip because you're a MaidThis franchisee and you need to make marketing stuff that looks like MaidThis — without hiring a designer or fighting with Canva.**

This is a **Claude skill**. You upload it to your Claude account once, then you can just chat with Claude like "make me a door hanger for a move-out promo in Scottsdale" and it will produce on-brand designs, copy, and marketing collateral for you.

---

## One-time setup (3 minutes)

1. Go to **claude.ai** and sign in (or create a free account).
2. Click **"Projects"** in the sidebar, then **"+ New project"**. Name it something like "MaidThis Marketing".
3. In that project, click **"Project knowledge"** (or the paperclip/attach icon depending on your plan).
4. Upload the **entire contents of this zip** — drag the whole unzipped folder in, or upload every file.
5. That's it. You're done.

## Using it

Open your MaidThis Marketing project and just ask. Examples that work great:

- *"Make me an Instagram post announcing our 100% Happiness Guarantee."*
- *"Design a flyer for a $50-off Deep Clean in the Austin market. My phone is 512-555-0199."*
- *"Write 5 Google Ads headlines for Airbnb hosts."*
- *"Design a door hanger — new-mover welcome, free first-visit discount."*
- *"Build a simple landing page for my Scottsdale market."*
- *"Draft a recruitment flyer for hiring more cleaners."*

Claude will read the brand rules, use the real logos and colors, and generate HTML you can screenshot, print, or send to a printer. It'll also offer a couple of variations — pick your favorite.

## Things Claude will automatically do right

- Use the correct logos (four variations included)
- Hit the voice — punchy, warm, never corporate
- Use the brand colors and Montserrat/Open Sans type
- Avoid banned phrases ("affordable", "reliable cleaning services", etc.)
- Leave room for your market name, phone number, and promo codes

## Things to tell Claude so it does even better

- Your market ("Scottsdale", "West LA", etc.)
- Your phone and website URL
- Any photos you have (upload them — Claude will use them instead of placeholders)
- Whether the output should be print (flyer, door hanger) or digital (social, email, web)

## If you don't want to use Claude

You can also go to **[brand.maidthis.com]** (corporate will share the exact link) — a simple web page where you can generate basic designs by filling out a short form. Less flexible than the skill, but works without an account.
